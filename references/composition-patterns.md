# Composition Patterns

## Purpose

Use a small set of stable composition patterns to keep the image light, consistent, and breathable.

Choose one pattern per image.
Do not combine multiple patterns.

## C01 - Corner Character + Open Space

Use when:
- theme is soft, reflective, emotional, or simple.

Layout:
- Place the young Chinese woman in the lower-left or lower-right corner.
- Leave the opposite upper area mostly empty.
- Add only 3-5 tiny props near the character.

Best for:
- slow life
- emotion sorting
- little things
- quiet reading

## C02 - Lower Character + Upper Negative Space

Use when:
- image needs a peaceful cover-like feeling without becoming a poster.

Layout:
- Put character in the lower third.
- Keep upper two-thirds mostly white or warm off-white.
- Tiny handwritten English note can float in the upper empty area.

Best for:
- sleep
- breathing
- calm mindset
- daily note

## C03 - Side Character + Opposite White Space

Use when:
- the scene needs a clear object or small table.

Layout:
- Place character and props on the left or right side.
- Leave the opposite side clean and open.
- Keep props close to the body.

Best for:
- reading
- study
- AI anxiety
- time planning

## C04 - Window Moment

Use when:
- the theme is quiet, alone, rainy, reflective, or slow.

Layout:
- Simple window shape on one side.
- Character sits or stands near it.
- Keep the window minimal, no detailed scenery.
- Leave white space around the scene.

Best for:
- alone time
- rainy day
- coffee and notes
- emotion sorting

## C05 - Small Table Moment

Use when:
- theme involves planning, study, work, reading, notes, or coffee.

Layout:
- Character sits beside a small table.
- Props sit on the table in a compact cluster.
- Avoid large desk, complex office, or multiple screens.

Best for:
- learning
- time management
- work-life balance
- AI anxiety

## C06 - Bedside Night Moment

Use when:
- theme is sleep, phone, rest, night routine.

Layout:
- Character near a bed or bedside table.
- Use moon, lamp, phone, small book.
- Keep night feeling gentle, not dark or cinematic.

Best for:
- sleep
- bedtime phone
- rest

## C07 - Rainy Commute Moment

Use when:
- theme is rain, commute, pressure, daily life.

Layout:
- Character holds an umbrella near a simple sign.
- Use 2-3 clouds or raindrops.
- No realistic city background.
- No dramatic weather.

Best for:
- rainy day commute
- life pressure
- small humor

## C08 - Tiny Symbol Support

Use when:
- theme is abstract but should remain light.

Layout:
- Character performs a simple action.
- Add 1-3 tiny symbols: heart, cloud, arrow, note, robot icon, moon.
- Symbols support meaning but do not become UI or infographic.

Best for:
- AI anxiety
- emotion整理
- decision making
- mindset

## Anti-patterns

Avoid:

- full-page poster composition
- centered hero model composition
- complex room scene
- complex office setup
- overhead flat lay
- multi-panel comic
- dense infographic
- big title area
- cinematic foreground/background depth
