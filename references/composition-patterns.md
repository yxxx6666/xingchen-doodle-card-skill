# Composition Patterns v0.8.10

Use one of these stable 3:4 card structures:

1. subject at lower-left, text at upper-right;
2. subject at lower-right, text at upper-left;
3. centered object or animal with text above and below;
4. split scene with one main comparison and a central text spine;
5. character-free infographic with one dominant visual symbol.

Keep generous margins and one reading direction. Do not force every page to use the same demographic or the same corner placement. Preserve series consistency through line style, palette, typography feeling and spacing rather than cloning the exact scene.
