# Chinese Doodle Editorial Style Lock v0.8.10

## Identity

- Chinese hand-drawn doodle editorial illustration;
- minimalist black ink line art;
- slightly imperfect sketch texture;
- soft pastel accents;
- white or warm off-white background;
- large negative space and clear information hierarchy;
- warm, calm and practical mood.

## Subject and scene

Derive the subject and setting from the approved content. Human, animal, object-led and character-free pages are all valid. Use one main action and minimal props. Do not force a young female character into every topic.

## Chinese text

Generate the approved Chinese title and body inside the same image. Critical title, number, unit, date and conclusion fields must be accurate and readable. Keep one consistent handwritten Chinese type feeling across pages.

## Canvas

Require native 3:4 portrait output. Use only controls supported by the current tool. Do not claim precise dimensions when they are unavailable.

## Prohibited

- anime, photorealism, 3D or cinematic poster styling;
- dense decoration, complex perspective or multi-action scenes;
- missing Chinese text or factual English decoration;
- local rendering, post-processing typography, compositing or fallback renderers.
