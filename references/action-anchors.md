# Action Anchors

## Purpose

The young Chinese woman must not be a passive decoration.
She must perform one clear, simple, daily action that carries the theme.

A good action anchor makes the image understandable even without title text.

## Rules

- Choose exactly one primary action.
- The action must be natural and quiet.
- The action must be drawable with minimal props.
- Avoid dramatic, exaggerated, heroic, commercial, or cinematic gestures.
- If the action needs too many props, simplify it.

## Approved action pool

### Sleep / phone / rest

- putting a phone onto a small bedside table
- closing a book before sleep
- turning off a small lamp
- sitting on the bed edge with a relaxed smile

### Reading / learning / knowledge

- reading a book by a small table
- writing one note on a sticky note
- underlining a short line in a notebook
- holding a book near a window

### AI anxiety / technology change

- looking calmly at a laptop
- writing a small note beside a laptop
- closing a laptop to take a breath
- placing a sticky note next to a simple robot icon

### Rain / commute / life pressure

- holding an umbrella beside a small sign
- walking slowly under tiny raindrops
- standing near a simple bus stop sign
- shaking rain from an umbrella gently

### Emotion / self-care

- writing feelings on sticky notes
- arranging a few papers on a desk
- watering a small plant
- sitting near a window and breathing calmly

### Time management

- checking a simple calendar
- writing one small plan
- moving a sticky note on a desk
- closing a notebook after planning

### Small things / daily life

- arranging books and a cup on a table
- placing a small flower in a cup
- folding one paper neatly
- cleaning a small desktop corner

### Slow life / alone time

- drinking coffee near a window
- holding a warm mug with both hands
- sitting quietly with a book
- looking out a window with a soft smile

## Bad action anchors

Avoid:

- standing like a poster model
- pointing dramatically at a big title
- flying, running, shouting, crying, fighting
- operating complex UI panels
- posing with luxury products
- looking like an anime heroine
- doing nothing while props carry all meaning

## Output requirement

When generating the final prompt, include the action in the Scene field.
