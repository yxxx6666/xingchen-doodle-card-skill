# Pose Safety v0.8.10

Use a simple pose that supports the page message.

Default:

- one main subject;
- one clear action;
- at most two interaction objects;
- relaxed seated, standing or side-facing pose;
- visible or logically placed limbs;
- no crossed multi-direction actions or severe occlusion.

For human figures, avoid duplicated hands, extra limbs, disconnected joints, impossible grip, floating objects and hands emerging from clothing or furniture.

The subject demographic must come from the content. Do not default every page to a young Chinese woman.
